Account — Profile

Self-service contact details for the signed-in user.

Introduction

Open Account → Profile from the user menu. This modal mirrors part of your local user record.

Editable fields

Full name is display-only here; administrators change it under Settings → Users. You may update Email and Mobile number for your own account, then press Save profile.
