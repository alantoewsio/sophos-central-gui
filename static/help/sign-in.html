Sign in and first-time setup

Local authentication for this application (separate from your Sophos Central login).

Introduction

This portal stores its own user accounts in an encrypted local database. You use these credentials to open the UI and call protected APIs. They are not the same as a Sophos Central administrator login.

First administrator password

On a fresh install, you may be prompted to set a password for the primary administrator account (or the earliest admin record). Passwords must meet the minimum length shown on the form. After saving, sign in with that username and password.

Additional accounts without passwords can be updated later under Settings → Users.

Signing in

Enter your local username and password. If your organization enabled idle timeout in Settings → General, inactive sessions end automatically and you will need to sign in again.
